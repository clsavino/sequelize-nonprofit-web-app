For OurMeals on WheelsProgram

Be an In-house Volunteer (takes about one hour) Transfer meals from one caddie to another for delivery each day Greet drivers Thank returning drivers and record any concerns Clean out caddies (simple wiping clean, airing out) — or — Be a Driver Deliver a hot meal to recipients between 10:30 a.m. and noon (A varying schedule is available Monday – Friday)

For OurSamaritan Relief Ministry

Be a Pantry Volunteer Greet donors, receive and document all donations Shelve donations Fill grocery orders according to information given to them by staff, and according to set grocery list Other tasks completed as needed such as inventory, personal pack assembly, working with fresh produce, cleaning, etc. A Pantry Volunteer should be able to bend and/or stoop easily, lift 15 lbs. and be able to grasp cans easily Times available Monday-Friday 9:00 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. or 1:30 p.m. – 5:00 p.m.

For OurThrift Shop

Be a Pricing Assistant Check garments for tears, broken zippers. Make sure garments are of good quality and fashionable Place garments on proper size hangers Use pricing guide to price garments and places price tags on them Hang up garments on the selling floor
